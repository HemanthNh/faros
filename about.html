Our origin

Why Faros?

Founded in Bengaluru, Faros (Greek for “lighthouse”) captures our role as steady navigators for founders, investors, and enterprises scaling new horizons. We recognized that clients needed legal partners who could move with deal pace, pre-empt regulatory issues, and provide crisp communication across stakeholders.

Our partners bring Big Law pedigree and in-house leadership experience. We intentionally built a lean structure that pairs senior involvement on every mandate with technology-enabled project management.

  • Outcome-first — advice framed around commercial decisions, not legal jargon.
  • Co-building — we work alongside CFOs, investment teams, and founders as embedded partners.
  • Accountability — transparent scopes, clear budgets, and proactive timelines.
