About Faros Legal

A Bengaluru-based corporate law firm with an international outlook and a commitment to clarity, precision, and service.

Our Story

Faros Legal was founded with a simple vision: deliver legal counsel that’s both commercially pragmatic and deeply meticulous. Our team brings diverse experience across sectors — from technology to real estate, media, manufacturing, and financial services — ensuring that every matter we handle is grounded in both legal and business realities.

We operate with a “professional yet personal” philosophy — building relationships where collaboration with clients leads to sharper, faster, and more sustainable outcomes.

Our Philosophy

Legal advice should not complicate business decisions — it should empower them. Our lawyers combine analytical rigor with strategic clarity to create value, minimize risk, and protect our clients’ interests in a fast-changing environment.

Client-Focused

Commercial Understanding

Responsiveness
